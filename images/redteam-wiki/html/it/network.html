VPN Access

VPN Server: 172.16.0.50
Protocol: OpenVPN (UDP 1194)
Config: Available from IT or jumpbox at /etc/openvpn/client.ovpn

To connect, get credentials from IT (usually matches AD account) and use OpenVPN client.
